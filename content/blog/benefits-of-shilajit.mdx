Shilajit, often called the "destroyer of weakness" in Sanskrit, is a sticky, tar-like substance found in the rocks of the Himalayas. Formed over centuries from the decomposition of plant matter, this mineral-rich resin has been prized in Ayurvedic medicine for over 3,000 years.

## How to Use Shilajit Effectively

### Dosage Guidelines
- **Beginners**: Start with a rice grain-sized amount (100-150mg)
- **Regular dose**: Pea-sized amount (300-500mg)
- **Frequency**: Once or twice daily

### Best Practices
1. **Dissolve in warm liquid**: Water, milk, or tea
2. **Take on empty stomach**: For optimal absorption
3. **Morning consumption**: For energy throughout the day
4. **Cycle usage**: 2-3 months on, 1 month off
